Fuel Delivery

If you run out of petrol, diesel or essential fluids like oil or coolant, our fuel delivery service will bring the supplies you need straight to your location. According to roadside assistance guides, drivers who run out of gas can have fuel delivered to them and simply pay for the cost of the fuel【395492010251814†L108-L111】. We’re here to make that process fast and hassle‑free.

Whether you’ve misjudged the distance to the next service station or have sprung a leak, we’ll ensure you get enough fuel to reach a garage safely. Our technicians are trained to handle fuel safely and will also check for leaks and advise on next steps.

What’s Included

  • Delivery of petrol or diesel to your stranded vehicle
  • Supply of essential fluids such as engine oil, coolant or brake fluid
  • No membership required – you pay only for the fuel delivered
  • 24/7 availability across South Africa
